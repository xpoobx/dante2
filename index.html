Dodge Challenger

When you picture an American Muscle Car, it's hard not to see the Dodge Challenger. The Dodge Challenger made its way into the market in 1969 as a 1970 model, and has been a staple of muscle cars ever since. The Challenger offers power, function, and style at a reasonable price point with a variety of models to choose from. The Challenger was discontinued after the release of the 2023 model, after more than 2 million units were created.

  • 303 - 807 Horsepower
  • 1969 - 2023
  • 0-60 -5.3s (2023 GT)
  • Max Speed (203mph)
